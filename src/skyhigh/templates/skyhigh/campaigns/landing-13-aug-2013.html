From business agility and ease of use, enterprise employees use cloud services for a variety of reasons. However, use of these services often comes with significant security, legal, and business risks, especially when they are procured and managed without IT’s involvement.

Gartner predicts that a full 35% of IT spending will take place outside of IT by 2015. Often referred to as “Shadow IT," low-cost subscription licenses and unknown apps are deployed, importing corporate data and integrating with other enterprise apps without IT having any involvement or even awareness.
