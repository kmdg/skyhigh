About Skyhigh

Skyhigh Networks enables our customers to accelerate the safe and profitable adoption of cloud services across their company.

We leverage over 8 decades of combined experience in cloud security and management, networking, data analysis and scalable cloud infrastructure to solve our customer’s most complex and pressing challenges. The result is the Skyhigh Cloud Services Manager, a game changing, zero footprint, highly available, service provider-scale, multi-tenant offering that provides complete visibility and control of the cloud.

Skyhigh and our leading partners help our customers safely unlock massive cloud returns by finding and eliminating risk, cost, and inefficiencies resulting from unmanaged "shadow IT" cloud adoption.

The Skyhigh Cloud Services Manager leverages a number of patented and unique technologies to provide unmatched visibility and control over Cloud service risk, usage, and costs. Skyhigh was a finalist for the RSA Conference 2013 Most Innovative Company award and was named a “Cool Vendor” by Gartner.
